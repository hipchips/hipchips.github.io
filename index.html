The HiPChips Conference

The International Workshop on

High Performance Chiplet and Interconnnect Architectures (HiPChips)

Our Mission

The HiPChips workshop series focuses on the latest research and development in chiplet and interconnect technologies, including architecture innovations, chip designs, software optimizations, and best deployment practices. It is intended to establish the tight connection between industry and acadmia, promoting the chiplet ecosystem for easy, large-scale adoption.
